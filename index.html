All about Academic Film and TV

We educate and inspire all to love and appreciate all aspects of film and television history. We to design educational and fun courses, talks and lectures on special request, please get in contact if you feel this suits your needs.

Film History

The history of film fascinates me, and I love to inspire those interested in the rich and complex history of how film came to look like it does today.

Film Education

Working in film for over 35 years has given me an incredible opportunity to learn about film, how restorative methods work and how movies are made.

Restoration Consultation

I have the tools and the expertise to restore films from many different formats, reversing many of the effects of time on analog print.

Who we are

My name is Tim Langley, and I am the founder and owner of Academic Film and Television. I must thank my mum and dad for inspiration at an early age, and for finding me that first job in film as a cinema projectionist with the Rank Organisations' Odeon Cinema chain when I left school in 1978. Film was a passion then. It's more than that now.
